What Makes a Good Solana Signal Bot?

A good signal bot should do three things:

1. Scan automatically. You shouldn't have to check charts manually. The bot should find trending tokens for you.

2. Score objectively. Raw data isn't enough. The bot should score tokens on clear metrics: momentum, volume, liquidity, market cap.

3. Assess risk. Every signal should include a risk level so you can make informed decisions.
