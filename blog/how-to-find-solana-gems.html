2. The 4 Metrics That Actually Matter

After analyzing thousands of Solana tokens, these are the metrics that predict short-term price movement:

  • 24h Price Change (Momentum) - Tokens with 10-200% gains in 24h tend to continue. Above 500% is usually a top signal.
  • Volume-to-Liquidity Ratio - A ratio of 2-15x means active trading relative to pool size. Below 1x is dead. Above 20x is unsustainable.
  • Liquidity - Minimum $50K for safety. Below that, you can't exit without massive slippage.
  • Market Cap - Sweet spot is $100K-$10M. Below $100K is too risky. Above $10M has less upside.

4. Red Flags to Avoid

  • Liquidity below $30K - you will get rekt on slippage
  • 24h change over 500% - likely a top, early buyers are dumping
  • Mint authority not revoked - creator can mint infinite tokens
  • Single liquidity pool - no redundancy means higher risk
  • Token age under 1 hour - too early, wait for confirmation
